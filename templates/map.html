How to Orient Yourself

  1. In a hallway, stand so that an odd number room is on your left, and an even number room is on your right.
  2. Now, forward from where you are standing is up on the map.
  3. Conversly, backward is now down on the map.

Tips and Tricks

  • Want to get to another floor? Navigate to the nearest staircase! Once you get to the floor you want, navigate from your current staircase to your destination.
  • Can't find what you're looking for? There are more than 3,000 people in this school happy to help you!
